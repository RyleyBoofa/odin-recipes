Guacamole Recipe

"Guacamole" by stu_spivack is licensed under CC BY-SA 2.0.

Description

Nachos without guacamole is like a car without wheels - what's the point? This homemade guacamole, made with ripe avacados and lime juice, gets a tasty kick from the cayenne pepper. It can be served chunky or smooth, depending on your tastes! Pair it with this nachos recipe for a quick and easy, yet delicious, dinner!

The original recipe can be found here.

Ingredients

Serving size: 2

  • 1-2 avacados, peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 tsp salt - or to taste
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup onion, diced
  • 1/2 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pinch cayenne pepper, ground (optional)

Steps

  1. Mash avacados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl.
  2. Mix in the tomatoes, onion, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper (if desired).
  3. Serve immediately if in a rush, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved falvour.

This guacamole recipe pairs perfectly with this nachos recipe!
