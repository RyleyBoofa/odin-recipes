McMuffins Recipe

"Sausage Egg Cheese Muffin" by Rex Roof is licensed under CC BY 2.0 license.

Description

Macca's breakfast is delicious - I would eat it everyday if I could. Although it's fair to say it's not the healthiest (or the cheapest) option. Well, introducing the perfect homemade McMuffin recipe. The star of the show is the homemade sausage patties, perfectly seasoned to mimic the real thing! Level up your breakfast game with these delicious homemade McMuffins!
The original recipe can be found here.

Ingredients

Serving size: 4

Sausage patties:

  • 500g ground pork (optionally swap for ground beef)
  • 1/2 tsp sage, dried, ground
  • 1/2 tsp thyme, dried
  • 1 tsp onion powder (optionally swap for garlic powder)
  • 3/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Muffins:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 English muffins
  • 4 slices cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 130°C.
  2. Place muffins on a baking trey, cut side up.

Sausage patties:

  1. Mix sausage patty ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Shape into 4 patties. Make them slightly larger than the muffins because they will shrink when cooking.
  3. Heat oil in a large non-stick skillet over high heat. Add patties and cook first side for 2-3 minutes, until brown. Flip them and cook the other side until brown. Don't forget to top them with cheese after flipping!

Eggs:

  1. Meanwhile, heat another pan over medium-high heat with 1 tbsp oil.
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan (use egg-rings if you have them). Add about 2 tbsp water into the skillet then cover with a lid. Cook for 1-2 minutes or until eggs are cooked to your liking.

Assemble the muffins in this order (bottom-to-top):

  1. Muffin bottom
  2. Sausage patty, topped with cheese
  3. Egg
  4. Muffin top (optionally add some sauce, if desired)

Now all that's left to do is enjoy your homemade McDonald's!
