Nachos Recipe

Description

This cheesy beef nachos recipe is just as delicious as it is cheap and easy to make! Loaded with seasoned beef mince, beans, salsa, and melted cheese, then topped with creamy guacamole and sour cream, it's the perfect quick and easy dinner. This recipe is adapted from from this Doritos Nachos Recipe.

Ingredients

Serving size: 2

  • 330g original corn chips
  • 1 cup medium salsa
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1/4 tsp ground chilli
  • 2 tsp ground cumin seed
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cup cheddar, shredded (colby jack or mexican cheese blend)

Optional toppings:

  • 1 green onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, snipped
  • 1/2 cup fresh jalapenos, sliced
  • 300ml sour cream
  • guacamole, homemade

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Arrange half of the corn chips on an 11 or 12-inch baking tray. Set aside.
  2. In a large skillet cook the onion & ground beef over medium heat until brown. Drain off fat.
  3. Add in the cumin, paprika and chilli, cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir the red kidney beans and cup of salsa into the beef and cook for a further 5 minutes.
  5. Spoon half the mixture over chips. Sprinkle with half the cheese. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes or until cheese just melts.
  6. Remove from oven. Top with remaining chips, beef mixture, and cheese. Bake for a further 10 minutes or until cheese has melted.
  7. Top with your desired additional toppings and medium salsa. Recommended toppings: fresh jalapenos, sour cream, and guacamole.
